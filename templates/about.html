FAQs

How accurate is BloodDetect?

With a clear fingerprint and parent info, accuracy can reach up to 98%. Results are reliable for most users.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All uploads are encrypted, processed securely, and never shared with third parties.

Do I need my parents' blood group?

No, but providing it helps the AI make even more accurate predictions for your blood group.

How fast are results?

You'll get your predicted blood group within seconds after uploading your fingerprint.
